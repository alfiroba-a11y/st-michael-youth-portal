The Holy Rosary & Separated Mysteries

Swipe or scroll horizontally through the distinct mysteries:

Joyful Mysteries (Monday & Saturday)

1. The Annunciation

The Angel Gabriel announces to Mary that she will conceive the Son of God.

2. The Visitation

Mary visits her cousin Elizabeth, who proclaims her blessed among women.

3. The Nativity

Jesus is born in a humble manger in Bethlehem.

4. The Presentation

The infant Jesus is presented in the Temple by Mary and Joseph.

5. Finding in the Temple

The 12-year-old Jesus is found discussing matters with the teachers.

Sorrowful Mysteries (Tuesday & Friday)

1. Agony in the Garden

Jesus sweats blood while praying in Gethsemane.

2. Scourging at the Pillar

Jesus is cruelly whipped by Roman soldiers.

3. Crowning with Thorns

A crown of sharp thorns is pressed onto the head of Jesus.

4. Carrying of the Cross

Jesus shoulders the heavy cross to Calvary.

5. The Crucifixion

Jesus dies on the cross for the salvation of humanity.

Way of the Cross (14 Stations)

Official sequential meditation stations arranged horizontally:

Station 1

Jesus is condemned to death.

Station 2

Jesus takes up His cross.

Station 3

Jesus falls the first time.

Station 4

Jesus meets His afflicted Mother.

Station 5

Simon of Cyrene helps carry the cross.

Station 6

Veronica wipes the face of Jesus.

Station 7

Jesus falls the second time.

Station 8

Jesus meets the women of Jerusalem.

Station 9

Jesus falls the third time.

Station 10

Jesus is stripped of garments.

Station 11

Jesus is nailed to the cross.

Station 12

Jesus dies on the cross.

Station 13

Jesus is taken down from the cross.

Station 14

Jesus is laid in the tomb.
