The Holy Rosary: Step-by-Step Guide & Mysteries

HOW TO PRAY THE ROSARY: 1. Make the Sign of the Cross and recite the Apostles’ Creed. 2. Pray 1 Our Father on the first large bead. 3. Pray 3 Hail Marys on the next three small beads for Faith, Hope, and Charity. 4. Pray 1 Glory Be. 5. Announce the mystery, pray 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, and the Fatima Prayer per decade.

Joyful Mysteries
  • 1st The Annunciation: The angel Gabriel announces to Mary that she will conceive the Son of God.
  • 2nd The Visitation: Mary visits her cousin Elizabeth, who proclaims her blessed.
  • 3rd The Nativity: Jesus is born in Bethlehem in a humble manger.
  • 4th The Presentation: The infant Jesus is presented in the Temple in Jerusalem.
  • 5th The Finding in the Temple: The young Jesus is found discussing the law with teachers in the Temple.
Sorrowful Mysteries
  • 1st The Agony in the Garden: Jesus prays in Gethsemane anticipating His passion.
  • 2nd The Scourging at the Pillar: Jesus is cruelly whipped by Roman soldiers.
  • 3rd The Crowning with Thorns: Soldiers mock Jesus, pressing a crown of thorns onto His head.
  • 4th The Carrying of the Cross: Jesus carries His heavy cross up to Mount Calvary.
  • 5th The Crucifixion: Jesus is nailed to the cross and dies for our sins.
Glorious Mysteries
  • 1st The Resurrection: Jesus rises triumphantly from the dead on the third day.
  • 2nd The Ascension: Jesus ascends bodily into Heaven before His disciples.
  • 3rd The Descent of the Holy Spirit: The Holy Spirit descends upon Mary and the Apostles as tongues of fire.
  • 4th The Assumption: The Blessed Virgin Mary is taken body and soul into heavenly glory.
  • 5th The Coronation of Mary: Mary is crowned Queen of Heaven and Earth.
Luminous Mysteries (Mysteries of Light)
  • 1st The Baptism in the Jordan: The Holy Spirit descends on Jesus as the Father proclaims Him His beloved Son.
  • 2nd The Wedding at Cana: Christ performs His first public miracle at Mary's request.
  • 3rd The Proclamation of the Kingdom: Jesus calls all people to conversion and forgiveness of sins.
  • 4th The Transfiguration: Jesus is transfigured in glory before Peter, James, and John.
  • 5th The Institution of the Eucharist: Jesus offers His Body and Blood at the Last Supper.
