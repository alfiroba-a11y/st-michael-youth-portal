The Holy Rosary: Step-by-Step Guide & Mysteries

HOW TO PRAY THE ROSARY: 1. Make the Sign of the Cross & Apostles' Creed. 2. Pray 1 Our Father, 3 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be. 3. Announce each mystery, 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, & Fatima Prayer.

Joyful Mysteries
  • 1st The Annunciation: Gabriel announces to Mary.
  • 2nd The Visitation: Mary visits Elizabeth.
  • 3rd The Nativity: Jesus is born in Bethlehem.
  • 4th The Presentation: Jesus is presented in the Temple.
  • 5th The Finding in the Temple: Jesus found among the teachers.
Sorrowful Mysteries
  • 1st The Agony in the Garden: Jesus prays in Gethsemane.
  • 2nd The Scourging at the Pillar: Jesus is whipped.
  • 3rd The Crowning with Thorns: Mocked with thorns.
  • 4th The Carrying of the Cross: Carries heavy cross to Calvary.
  • 5th The Crucifixion: Jesus dies on the cross.
Glorious Mysteries
  • 1st The Resurrection: Jesus rises triumphantly.
  • 2nd The Ascension: Jesus ascends into Heaven.
  • 3rd The Descent of the Holy Spirit: Pentecost fire.
  • 4th The Assumption: Mary taken into heavenly glory.
  • 5th The Coronation of Mary: Queen of Heaven.
Luminous Mysteries
  • 1st Baptism in the Jordan: Heavens open.
  • 2nd Wedding at Cana: First public sign.
  • 3rd Proclamation of the Kingdom: Call to conversion.
  • 4th The Transfiguration: Transfigured in glory.
  • 5th Institution of the Eucharist: Last Supper.
